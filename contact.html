Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide warranty on refurbished laptops? ▼

Yes! All our refurbished laptops come with 1–6 months warranty depending on the model. We honour all warranty claims with no questions asked.

How long does laptop repair take? ▼

Most common repairs like screen replacement, OS installation, SSD upgrade, and virus removal are done the same day. Complex motherboard repairs may take 2–3 days.

Can I exchange my old laptop? ▼

Yes, we accept old laptops for exchange. WhatsApp us with photos and specs of your old laptop and we'll give you the best exchange value towards your new purchase.

Do you offer home pickup for repairs? ▼

Yes, for customers in Vijayawada city limits, we offer pickup and delivery for laptop repairs on request. Contact us on WhatsApp to arrange a pickup.
