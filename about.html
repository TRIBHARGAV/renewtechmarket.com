📖 Our Story

From a Small Shop to Guntur's Most Trusted Tech Store

Renew Tech Market began with a simple belief: technology should be accessible to everyone. Founded over 5 years ago in Guntur, our mission was to bridge the gap between quality computers and affordability.

What started as a small repair shop quickly grew into Guntur's go-to destination for refurbished laptops. We source, test, refurbish, and sell laptops after rigorous 100-point quality checks — ensuring every device we sell performs like new but costs a fraction of the price.

Today, we've served 500+ happy customers — students, professionals, businesses, and schools — all trusting us for quality tech at honest prices.
